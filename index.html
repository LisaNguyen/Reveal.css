Voila.css

Definition: there it is; there you are.

Voila.css is a small but growing collection of quirky animations to pair with images and their corresponding text. It adds a bit of pizzazz to how text is revealed to the reader and hopefully, will draw some wows and woahs in the process.

Photos are from Unsplash and text is from Kanye-Ipsum
